Magic Avatar

Figma Plugin

Avatar Magic Plugin - a super handy tool that allows designers to select any number of frames or shapes of any size and generate random high-quality images with just one click. It's been a fun project to work on and some errors just roam around my head. This plugin used Unsplash API and Figma's plugin API.

Plugin purpose and Goal

This tool be a game-changer when it comes to reducing the time designers spend searching for avatars that not only avoid repetition but also maintain high quality, all within a matter of seconds. In essence, it streamlines the entire process, allowing designers to focus more on their creative work and less on the time-consuming task of finding suitable images.And developing a third-party plugin made a strong foundation to read docs, work with their methods and properties, and available options. By the way, Gained a deeper understanding of how to effectively interact with external systems and APIs
